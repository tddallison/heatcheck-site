HeatCheck Privacy Policy

Effective: June 12, 2026

HeatCheck does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

What the app stores, and where: Swimmer profiles you create (name, optional age, optional team code) and meet schedules parsed from heat sheet PDFs you provide are stored only on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. HeatCheck has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising.

Heat sheet PDFs: Parsing happens entirely on your phone. The PDF and its contents never leave your device.

Children: HeatCheck is intended for use by parents and adults. Swimmer profiles (which may describe children) exist only on the parent's device and are never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Team theme codes: Sharing a theme generates a short text code containing only color values and a team abbreviation. It contains no personal information.

Data deletion: Deleting the app permanently deletes all data.
